About Oswal Motors

Welcome to Oswal Motors, your trusted partner in the automotive industry for nearly two decades. We have been serving our community with pride and dedication since 2006, providing quality used vehicles that our customers can rely on.

Our team of experienced professionals carefully inspects every vehicle to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and safety. We believe in transparency and honesty in all our dealings, which has earned us the trust of thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

At Oswal Motors, we don't just sell cars; we build lasting relationships with our customers. Our commitment to exceptional service continues even after you drive off the lot, with comprehensive warranty support and after-sales service.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the best quality used vehicles at fair prices, backed by honest advice and reliable after-sales support.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred used car dealership in Sri Lanka, known for integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, customer focus, and a commitment to excellence guide everything we do at Oswal Motors.
